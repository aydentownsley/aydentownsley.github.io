About Me
I am a young Tulsa creative, with a passion for finding innovative ways to use tech! I am attending Holberton to tackle software development head on and learn about new technologies. I am starting my second year and will be specializing in Augmented and Virtual Reality, but that is far from my only software focus. In addition to school I am a hobbyist videographer and have had a blast telling stories through video and photos. I try to bring this skill into my work whenever I can. (Just check out the teaser trailer for Rage Chess...). Above all, I love to tell stories, and I will do so in any medium I can get my hands on. I look forward to helping you tell your story, wether it be with tech, words, or images. Feel free to contanct me to dream a little about your latest project!
