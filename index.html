About Me
I am a young Tulsa creative, with a passion for finding innovative ways to use tech! I am attending Holberton to tackle software development head on and learn about new technologies. I am specializing in Augmented and Virtual Reality. I also have a thing for fun aesthetics (hence the retro web1 site) In addition to school I am a hobbyist videographer and have had a blast telling stories through any medium. I try to bring these skills into my work whenever I can. (Just check out the teaser trailer for Rage Chess...). Above all, I love to tell stories, and I will do so in any medium I can get my hands on. I look forward to helping you tell your story, wether it be with tech, words, or images. Feel free to contanct me to dream a little about your latest project!
Projects
Rage Chess is a VR experience for people who might just kind of hate losing. It is an ongoing project that includes an online mulitplayer experience as well as utilizing the chess engine, stockfish, to allow for solo play! I got to work with some phenomenal people on this project and had a blast digging into all things VR. In fact, this very project is what really caused me to fall in love with XR. I started working on this game and quickly bought a Quest 2 so I could spend more time in the space. I am excited to continue to learn and advance my skills. Currently I am working on the solo play aspect of Rage Chess. It has been a very cool experience translating 3D data into a parsable string for a uci chess engine like stockfish. Download it Here!
XR stands for any sort of modified reality. In addition to being able to work in Virtual Reality, like with Rage Chess, I have also cultivated skills in Augmented Reality. This project is a business card that uses a unique image as a target as a home point for a virtual work and layers that digital object with the physical world. Above you can see that layout as it appears in the Unity Project. If you Click Here you can see a quick demo of the working model.
